The Lala Fútbol Club secured its ticket to the First Division of Venezuelan football after beating Margarita 2-0 and to overcome the global final of the Clausura Tournament of the second category (2-1), to become champions.
The meeting, played on a deteriorated turf of the Cachamay Total Entertainment Center, saw how a Guyanese cast played the first leg down (1-0) and relied on two figures throughout the direct qualification rounds to achieve their goal: Ángel Hernández and Abizail Lugo.
